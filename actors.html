SOME CELEBRATED ACTORS OF BOLLYWOOD

Rajesh Khanna

Rajesh Khanna was a legendary Indian actor known as the first superstar of Hindi cinema. His films were admired for their emotional depth, romantic appeal, and memorable music, making him a cultural icon of his time. He enjoyed an unprecedented fan following and delivered a historic run of consecutive hit films during the late 1960s and early 1970s. He received several prestigious awards, including multiple Filmfare Awards, and is regarded as one of the most influential figures in Indian cinema, with iconic films like Anand, Aradhana, Haathi Mere Saathi, The Train and Kati Patang.
